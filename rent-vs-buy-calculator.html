Rent vs Buy FAQ

What costs are included in buying?

Mortgage payments, down payment opportunity cost, closing costs (~3%), property taxes, and maintenance. Equity gained through appreciation offsets these.

When does buying make financial sense?

Generally if you plan to stay 5+ years and live in a market with steady appreciation. The longer you stay, the more buying tends to win financially.
