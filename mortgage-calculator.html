Frequently Asked Questions

How is a mortgage payment calculated?

Monthly payment = P × [r(1+r)ⁿ] / [(1+r)ⁿ - 1], where P = principal, r = monthly interest rate, n = total payments. This calculator handles the math instantly.

Does this include taxes and insurance?

No — this calculates Principal & Interest (P&I) only. Your actual payment will be higher if you include property taxes and homeowner's insurance (PITI).

What is a good mortgage rate?

Rates vary by market conditions and your credit score. As of 2026, 30-year fixed rates typically range between 6–7.5%. A higher credit score and larger down payment usually secure lower rates.
