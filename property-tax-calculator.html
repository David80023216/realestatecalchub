Property Tax FAQ

What is a homestead exemption?

A homestead exemption reduces the taxable value of your primary residence, lowering your annual property tax bill. Many states offer this to owner-occupants.

How are property taxes paid?

Most homeowners pay property taxes through an escrow account as part of their monthly mortgage payment (PITI). You can also pay directly to your county.
